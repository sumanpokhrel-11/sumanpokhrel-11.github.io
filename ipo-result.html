How to Check IPO Result in Nepal (Step by Step)

1
Find Your BOID
Your 16-digit BOID is in MeroShare app, your bank's DEMAT portal, or on your DEMAT certificate.
2
Visit CDSC Portal
Go to iporesult.cdsc.com.np — the official and only authoritative source for Nepal IPO allotment results.
3
Select Company
Choose the company whose IPO you applied for from the dropdown. Results are only available after CDSC publishes them.
4
Enter BOID & Check
Enter your 16-digit BOID, complete the captcha, and click View Result. You'll see allotted or not allotted status.

Understanding Nepal IPO Allotment Process

Nepal's IPO (Initial Public Offering) system is regulated by SEBON and operated through CDSC's C-ASBA platform. All applications go through MeroShare and are processed digitally.

TopicDetails
Minimum Application10 kitta (shares) at Rs 100 face value = Rs 1,000 minimum
Allotment MethodComputerized lottery when oversubscribed; all get shares if undersubscribed
Result Timeline7–10 working days after IPO closing date
Refund TimelineWithin 15 working days for unsuccessful applicants
Kitta per ApplicantMinimum 10 kitta per successful applicant in lottery
Where to Checkiporesult.cdsc.com.np or MeroShare portal
What You Need16-digit BOID number (from your DEMAT/MeroShare account)
Common Rejection ReasonsMultiple applications from same PAN, insufficient funds, wrong details
CDSC Annual FeeRs 50/year — ensure it's paid or your DEMAT may be inactive
After AllotmentShares credited to DEMAT, listed on NEPSE after 7–10 days
